About Phoebe

I'm a writer and editor with a creative approach to communication. Words, art, music--these all come together to form the most persuasive rhetoric humankind knows. As a writer, artist, and musician, I consider myself uniquely suited to meet the needs of your audience; even if we don't add a visual or auditory dimension to your dream, we'll be working together with an awareness of the creative possibilities.

To check out my creative work outside of writing and editing, read my blog, visit my YouTube channel, or find me in my kitchen!
